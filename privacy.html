Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

"Karma en Visto" (the "App") is a personal content-automation tool that publishes short-form videos to our own social media accounts (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram). This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is used.

1. Information we access

When you authorize the App through TikTok Login Kit, the App accesses, only for the authorizing account (our own brand account):

  • Basic profile information (username and avatar), shown before publishing as required by TikTok.
  • Permission to upload and publish videos to that same account (video.upload, video.publish scopes).

The App does not access, collect, or store data belonging to any other TikTok user.

2. How we use the information

The information is used solely to upload and publish our own videos to our own account. We do not use it for advertising, profiling, or any other purpose.

3. Data storage and security

OAuth access and refresh tokens are stored privately on our own systems and are used only to authenticate publishing requests. Tokens are never shared with third parties.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. We do not transfer data to anyone other than the platform APIs (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram) required to publish our content.

5. Data retention and deletion

You may revoke the App's access at any time from your TikTok account settings (Security & permissions > Apps). Upon revocation, stored tokens become invalid and are deleted. To request deletion of any data, contact us below.

6. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

7. Changes

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date will reflect changes.
